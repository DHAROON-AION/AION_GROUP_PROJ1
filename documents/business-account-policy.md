# AION Bank — Business Current Account

## Target Customers
Registered businesses, sole proprietorships, and partnerships operating in the GCC.

## Minimum Opening Balance
SAR 10,000 for standard business accounts. SAR 100,000 for premium business banking.

## Transaction Limits
- Daily transfer limit: SAR 500,000 (standard), SAR 2,000,000 (premium)
- Cheque book: 25 leaves included; additional books at SAR 50 each

## Required Documents
- Valid trade/commercial license
- Memorandum of Association
- Board resolution for account opening
- Authorized signatory IDs
- Proof of business address

## Processing
Account opening typically completes within 3 business days after document verification.
