## 1. Daily Transaction Limits by Channel
The following limits define the maximum allowable cumulative transfer amounts within a rolling 24-hour window per customer account tier.

### 1.1 Retail Standard Tier
* Internet Banking Daily Limit: 50,000 SAR
* Mobile Banking Daily Limit: 50,000 SAR
* ATM Transfer Daily Limit: 20,000 SAR
* QR Code Payment Daily Limit: 10,000 SAR

### 1.2 Retail Premium/VIP Tier
* Internet Banking Daily Limit: 200,000 SAR
* Mobile Banking Daily Limit: 200,000 SAR
* ATM Transfer Daily Limit: 50,000 SAR
* QR Code Payment Daily Limit: 30,000 SAR

---

## 2. Limits by Transfer Type (Standard Accounts)
Regardless of the digital channel used, individual transaction caps apply based on the relationship type of the destination account.

* Intra-Bank Transfer (Own Accounts): Unlimited
* Intra-Bank Transfer (Third-Party): 100,000 SAR per transaction
* Local Inter-Bank Transfer (Instant / IPS): 20,000 SAR per transaction
* Local Inter-Bank Transfer (Standard SARIE): 50,000 SAR per transaction
* International SWIFT Transfer: 25,000 SAR equivalent per transaction

---

## 3. Temporary Limit Enhancements
Customers may temporarily elevate their daily transfer limits via the mobile application or internet banking portal subject to the following criteria:
* Maximum temporary elevation cap: 150% of standard tier limits.
* Validity window: Maximum of 48 hours from the time of approval.
* Authentication requirement: Mandatory completion of Biometric Verification combined with a time-based One-Time Password (TOTP).
