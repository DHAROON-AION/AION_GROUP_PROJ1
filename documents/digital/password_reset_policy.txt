## 1. Password Complexity Requirements
When a user resets their password, the new string must pass the local regex validation rules before being hashed (Argon2id) and stored in the on-premise database:
* Minimum Length: 12 characters.
* Composition: Must contain at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (!@#$%^&*).
* History Rule: Cannot match any of the previous 5 passwords used.

## 2. Self-Service Reset Workflow
If a user forgets their password, they must complete a multi-factor reset flow:
* Step 1: Input valid National ID or Iqama number.
* Step 2: Input active Debit Card number and the corresponding 4-digit physical ATM PIN.
* Step 3: Validate the OTP sent to the registered mobile number.
* Step 4: Define and confirm the new complex password.
