# AION Bank — SME Lending Policy

## SME Definition
Small and Medium Enterprises with annual revenue between SAR 1 million and SAR 40 million.

## Loan Products
- Working capital facilities
- Equipment financing
- Trade finance (letters of credit, guarantees)

## Credit Assessment
Relationship managers evaluate:
- Debt service coverage ratio (minimum 1.25x)
- Current ratio (minimum 1.0x)
- Cash flow trends (6-12 months)
- Sector outlook

## Collateral
Secured facilities require collateral coverage of 100-150% of exposure depending on asset type.

## Early Warning Indicators
Accounts are flagged for review when:
- Two or more consecutive months of negative cash flow
- Sector deterioration signal exceeds 0.65
- Payment delays exceed 15 days

## Human Review Requirement
Any provision increase or facility downgrade requires relationship manager and credit committee approval.
