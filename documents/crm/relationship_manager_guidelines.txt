## 1. Core Portfolio Management Rules
Relationship Managers (RMs) assigned to Premium and VIP portfolios are legally obligated to review their assigned clients' portfolios quarterly to ensure ongoing suitability.

### 1.1 Mandatory Interaction Cadence
* VIP Tier Clients: Minimum of one secure, recorded audio/video interaction or face-to-face meeting every 30 calendar days.
* Premium Tier Clients: Minimum of one formal relationship health check via secure digital message or email every 60 calendar days.

## 2. Security and Device Compliance
* RMs are strictly forbidden from copying client financial projections, asset balances, or contact logs onto local desktop storage or unencrypted media.
* Any data exported for portfolio analysis must remain within the secure, sandboxed container network allocated to the wealth management stack.
