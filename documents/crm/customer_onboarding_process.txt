## 1. Onboarding Workflow Overview
The bank executes a multi-stage validation workflow for all new relationship originations to ensure complete alignment with data privacy laws and zero leakage of customer data outside local infrastructure.

[Stage 1: Identity Capture] -> [Stage 2: KYC & Verification] -> [Stage 3: Consent & PDPL Sign-off] -> [Stage 4: Core Account Provisioning]

2. Mandatory Document Checklists
2.1 Individual National Residents
Valid National ID / Iqama Card (Captured via secure local optical character recognition)

Verified Mobile Number (Linked to national digital identity single sign-on)

Proof of Physical Address (National Address Registry record)

Employment Verification Letter or Salary Certificate (For active checking accounts)

2.2 Data Sovereignty and Consent Mandate
Before account provisioning, the relationship manager or digital platform must present the formal Data Privacy Disclosure.

Customer must explicitly opt-in via a cryptographic digital signature.

All telemetry, personal identifiable information (PII), and financial history captured during onboarding must reside exclusively in on-premise secure database clusters.

Cross-border transit of onboarding records is strictly prohibited.
