# AION Bank — KYC and AML Compliance

## Know Your Customer
All account holders must complete KYC verification at onboarding and every 2 years thereafter.

## Required Information
- Full legal name and date of birth
- National ID or passport
- Source of funds declaration for transactions above SAR 100,000
- Beneficial ownership for corporate accounts

## Politically Exposed Persons
Enhanced due diligence applies to PEPs and their associates per SAMA regulations.

## Suspicious Activity
Staff must report suspicious transactions to the compliance unit within 24 hours. Customers are not notified of SAR filings.

## Sanctions Screening
All customers and transactions are screened against OFAC, UN, and local sanctions lists in real time.

## Record Retention
KYC records retained for minimum 10 years after account closure per regulatory requirements.
