# AION Bank — Personal Loan Product Guide

## Eligibility
- Minimum age: 21 years
- Minimum monthly salary: SAR 5,000 for salaried customers
- Maximum debt-burden ratio: 45% of gross monthly income

## Loan Amounts
Personal loans range from SAR 10,000 to SAR 500,000 based on credit assessment.

## Tenure
Loan tenure options: 12, 24, 36, 48, or 60 months.

## Interest Rates
Indicative APR ranges from 5.5% to 12.5% depending on credit profile, employer category, and loan tenure.

## Required Documents
- Salary certificate (dated within 30 days)
- Bank statements (3 months)
- National ID copy
- Employer verification letter

## Processing Time
Standard processing: 3-5 business days after complete documentation.
