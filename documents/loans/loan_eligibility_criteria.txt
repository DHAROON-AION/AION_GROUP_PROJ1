## 1. Pre-Disbursement Verification Checklist
Before the core banking system automatically releases approved loan funds into a customer's account, the following digital checks must pass without human intervention:
* Contract Signature: The promissory note and loan contract must be cryptographically signed by the user via the National Single Sign-On (Nafath) portal.
* Salary Assignment: For salary-backed loans, the system must verify the receipt of the electronic "Salary Assignment Letter" from the employer's HR system via the secure B2B gateway.
* Insurance Binding: For auto and home loans, the mandatory collateral insurance policy must be active and registered in the database.

## 2. Disbursement Execution
* Personal Loans: Funds are disbursed instantly to the customer's linked internal Current Account once the checklist passes.
* Auto Loans: Funds are never disbursed to the customer. The system generates a manager's cheque or electronic direct transfer payable exclusively to the verified automotive dealership.
* Home Loans: Disbursements are scheduled in tranches directly to the property developer (for off-plan) or as a single lump sum to the seller's verified IBAN (for ready properties) under the supervision of a public notary.
