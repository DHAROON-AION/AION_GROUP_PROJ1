## 1. Product Parameters
The Personal Finance product is an unsecured cash facility based on the Sharia principle of Tawarruq (Commodity Murabaha).
* Maximum Tenure: Strictly capped at 60 months (5 years) by central bank mandate. No exceptions.
* Maximum Financing Amount: Up to 2,500,000 SAR, strictly constrained by the customer's maximum eligible DBR.
* Profit Rate: Fixed for the entire duration of the tenure. The rate is dynamically assigned during the application based on the customer's risk profile, employer tier, and requested tenure.

## 2. Refinancing and Top-Ups
* A customer may apply for a "Top-Up" (refinancing the existing loan to extract additional equity) only after successfully paying down 20% of the original principal balance.
* During a Top-Up, the old contract is closed, early settlement formulas are applied, and a new 60-month contract is generated covering the remaining balance plus the newly requested funds.
