## 1. Auto Lease-to-Own Criteria
The bank offers vehicle financing under a Sharia-compliant Ijarah (Lease-to-Own) structure.
* Maximum Tenure: 60 months (5 years) for brand new vehicles; 36 months for pre-owned vehicles.
* Down Payment: 5% minimum for salary-assigned customers; 10% minimum for non-salary assigned.
* Maximum Financing Amount: 500,000 SAR or restricted by the maximum 45% Debt Burden Ratio (DBR), whichever is lower.
* Insurance: Comprehensive motor insurance is bundled for the entire tenure and calculated into the monthly lease payment.

## 2. Early Settlement and Transfer
* Early Settlement: A customer can request early ownership transfer by settling the outstanding principal. The bank will waive the remaining unearned profit minus a processing fee equivalent to 3 months of profit.
* Transfer of Lease: The lease cannot be transferred to a third party without the bank re-evaluating the new lessee's credit profile and issuing a new contract.
