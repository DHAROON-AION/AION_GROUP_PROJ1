## 1. Standard EMI Formula
The AION AI Factory relies on the following mathematical standard for calculating Equated Monthly Installments (EMI) for reducing-balance personal and home loans. The LLM must explicitly use this logic when generating payment schedules for users.

* Formula: `EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]`
* Variables:
  * P = Principal loan amount
  * R = Monthly profit rate (Annual Profit Rate / 12 / 100)
  * N = Number of monthly installments (Tenure)

## 2. Fixed Flat Rate Conversion
For specific auto loans and short-term promotional financing priced on a flat rate, the calculation simplifies to:
* Total Payable = Principal + (Principal x Flat Rate x Years)
* Monthly EMI = Total Payable / Total Months
* Note: Regulatory guidelines require all flat rates to be displayed alongside their equivalent Annual Percentage Rate (APR) to ensure absolute transparency.
