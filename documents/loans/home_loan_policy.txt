## 1. Mortgage Financing Rules
The bank offers Murabaha and Ijarah real estate financing for ready properties and off-plan units approved by the Wafi program.
* Maximum Tenure: 300 months (25 years).
* Maximum Financing Limit: 5,000,000 SAR.
* Age Restriction: The customer's age at the maturity of the loan cannot exceed 65 years for standard employees, or 70 years for military and government personnel.
* Repayment Cap: The EMI cannot exceed 60% of the customer's net verified monthly income.

## 2. Joint Financing
* First-degree relatives (spouses, parents, children) can apply as co-applicants to pool their income and increase the maximum financing limit.
* In joint financing, a mandatory life insurance policy is issued for both applicants, prorated based on their income contribution percentage.
