## 1. SME Financing Parameters
This policy governs working capital and term loans provided to Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs).
* Eligibility: The business must possess a valid Commercial Registration (CR), be actively operating for a minimum of 2 continuous years, and demonstrate positive cash flow in the audited financials for the preceding year.
* Facility Limits: Unsecured working capital limits up to 1,000,000 SAR. Secured term loans up to 10,000,000 SAR.
* Profit Rates: Priced dynamically based on the company's internal risk rating (1 through 7) assessed via the local predictive credit model.

## 2. Required Documentation
* Audited financial statements by a certified public accountant.
* Bank statements covering the last 12 months (if the primary operating account is held externally).
* Personal guarantees from all partners/shareholders holding more than a 10% stake in the entity.
