# AION Bank — Credit Card Terms and Benefits

## Card Types
1. **Classic Card** — No annual fee first year, 1% cashback on groceries
2. **Gold Card** — SAR 500 annual fee, 1.5% cashback, travel insurance
3. **Platinum Card** — SAR 1,200 annual fee, 2% cashback, lounge access, concierge

## Credit Limits
Limits range from SAR 5,000 to SAR 150,000 based on income and credit history.

## Payment Terms
Minimum payment: 5% of outstanding balance or SAR 100, whichever is higher.
Payment due date: 25 days from statement date.

## Foreign Transactions
Foreign exchange markup: 2.5% above prevailing rate on international transactions.

## Lost or Stolen Cards
Report immediately via phone banking or mobile app. Cards are blocked within minutes and replacement issued within 3-5 business days.
