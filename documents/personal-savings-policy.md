# AION Bank — Personal Savings Account Policy

## Overview
AION Bank offers personal savings accounts with competitive profit rates for GCC residents.

## Minimum Balance
- Standard savings: SAR 1,000 minimum balance
- Premium savings: SAR 50,000 minimum balance

## Profit Rates
Profit rates are calculated on daily balances and credited monthly. Current indicative rates range from 1.5% to 3.2% depending on tier and balance.

## Withdrawals
Customers may make up to 6 free withdrawals per month. Additional withdrawals incur a SAR 25 fee per transaction.

## Account Opening
Valid national ID or passport, proof of address, and initial deposit are required. Account activation typically completes within 2 business days.
