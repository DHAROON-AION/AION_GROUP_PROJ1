# AION Bank — Wealth Management Services

## Investment Advisory
Licensed advisors provide portfolio recommendations aligned with customer risk profiles: conservative, balanced, or growth.

## Minimum Investment
SAR 100,000 for managed portfolios. SAR 500,000 for private banking services.

## Product Range
- Fixed income funds
- Equity funds (regional and global)
- Sukuk portfolios
- Structured deposits

## Fees
Advisory fee: 0.5% to 1.0% annually on assets under management.
Performance fees may apply to alternative investments.

## Suitability
All investment recommendations require completion of a suitability assessment. High-risk products are restricted to qualified investors.

## Disclaimer
Investments carry risk. Past performance does not guarantee future returns. Customers should review prospectuses before investing.
