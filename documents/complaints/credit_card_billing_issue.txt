## 1. Valid Chargeback Conditions
A customer can initiate a formal chargeback process if the transaction meets one of the following conditions:
* Billed for a canceled subscription (Requires proof of cancellation notice provided to merchant).
* Goods/services not delivered by the promised date.
* Billed amount differs significantly from the receipt/invoice (Overcharge).
* Defective or counterfeit merchandise received.

## 2. Chargeback Processing Rules
* Time Limit: Chargeback claims must be submitted within 120 days of the transaction settlement date.
* Document Requirement: Customers must upload supporting evidence (receipts, merchant correspondence) directly into the secure app environment.
* Interest Suspension: Once a dispute is successfully logged, the core billing system automatically pauses interest accumulation on the disputed amount until the investigation concludes.
