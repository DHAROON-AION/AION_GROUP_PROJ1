## 1. Complaint Logging Categories
To ensure accurate semantic retrieval and reporting, branch service complaints must be classified into one of the following strict categories during intake:
* Staff Unprofessionalism (Requires exact timestamp and agent desk number).
* Excessive Wait Times (Triggered if wait exceeds 45 minutes from ticket issuance).
* Physical Infrastructure (E.g., broken self-service kiosks, unsafe conditions).
* Transaction Refusal (Requires citation of the specific product/service denied).

## 2. Escalation and Investigation Protocol
* Complaints are routed directly to the Regional Branch Director, bypassing the local Branch Manager.
* CCTV and Audio Logs: The investigation unit is authorized to pull on-premise security recordings corresponding to the customer's visit timestamp.
* Customer Callback: The Regional Director's office must contact the customer via recorded telephone line within 3 business days to provide a resolution summary.
