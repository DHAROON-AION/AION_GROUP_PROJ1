## 1. Expected Clearing Cycles
Before logging a fault, the support system must verify the standard clearing cycle parameters:
* Intra-Bank Cheques (Drawn on the same bank): Clears instantly upon deposit validation.
* Local Inter-Bank Cheques (Deposited before 12:00 PM AST): Clears by 4:00 PM the next business day.
* Local Inter-Bank Cheques (Deposited after 12:00 PM AST): Clears by 4:00 PM on the second business day.

## 2. Handling Stuck or Returned Instruments
* Technical Rejection: If the centralized optical character recognition (OCR) system fails to read the MICR line, the cheque is flagged for manual review, adding 24 hours to the SLA.
* Insufficient Funds (Bounced): The system immediately applies a 50 SAR penalty to the issuer. The depositor receives a digital notification and must collect the physical returned cheque from their domiciled branch.
