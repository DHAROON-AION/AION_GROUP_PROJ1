## 1. Immediate Triage & Card Freeze Actions
When a customer contacts support or inputs a claim via the chatbot regarding an unrecognized or unauthorized debit from their account, the agent/system must execute the following sequence:

### Step 1: Immediate Token Deactivation
Instantly block the associated debit card, credit card, or digital wallet token tokenized on the mobile app. 

### Step 2: Session Invalidation
Force-terminate all active internet banking sessions and mobile applications linked to the customer's unique CIF number to halt further exploit capabilities.

---

## 2. Dispute Logging and Provisional Credit Rules
* Timeline to file: Customers must report unauthorized transactions within 45 days of the statement issue date.
* Dispute Investigation Window: Standard investigations must be finalized within 14 business days.
* Provisional Credit: For transactions exceeding 1,000 SAR where fraud is highly probable and no gross negligence (e.g., sharing PIN or OTP) is detected on the customer's part, a temporary provisional credit for the disputed amount must be applied to the account within 48 hours while final analysis finishes.
