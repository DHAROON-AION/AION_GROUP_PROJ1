## 1. Primary Causes for Automated Card Blocks
A debit card is automatically flagged with a "Restricted" status (Code 62) in the core PostgreSQL database under the following conditions:
* PIN Retries: Three consecutive incorrect PIN attempts at an ATM or POS terminal.
* Suspicious Activity: Algorithmic detection of impossible travel velocities (e.g., physical transactions in two different countries within a 1-hour window).
* Account Dormancy: The linked primary account transitions to a dormant state (731+ days of inactivity).

## 2. Unblocking Procedures
* PIN Lock: Customers can reset their PIN and unblock the card instantly via the self-hosted mobile application by passing biometric verification and inputting a One-Time Password (OTP).
* Fraud Lock: If blocked by the automated fraud engine, the card cannot be unblocked digitally. The customer must verbally confirm the flagged transactions with the Tier-2 fraud team via the secure telephony system.
