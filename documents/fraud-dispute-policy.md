# AION Bank — Fraud and Dispute Resolution

## Reporting Fraud
Report suspicious transactions immediately via:
- Phone banking hotline (24/7)
- Mobile app "Report Fraud" feature
- Nearest branch

## Investigation Timeline
- Card fraud: provisional credit within 5 business days
- Full investigation: 30-45 business days
- Wire transfer disputes: 10 business days initial review

## Customer Responsibilities
Customers must report unauthorized transactions within 30 days of statement date. Failure to report promptly may limit recovery.

## Chargeback Process
For merchant disputes, submit dispute form with supporting documentation within 60 days of transaction date.

## Data Protection
All fraud reports are handled under PDPL and SAMA guidelines. Customer data is never shared with third parties without consent.
