## 1. Cross-Border Authorizations
To mitigate international fraud exposure, all physical debit and credit cards are restricted to domestic (Saudi Arabia) use by default upon issuance. 

* Customer Activation: Users must explicitly enable "International Travel Mode" via the digital banking portal before traveling.
* Duration: Travel mode can be activated for a maximum rolling window of 90 days, after which it automatically disables.

## 2. Geographic Blocklists
* Transactions originating from countries designated as high-risk by national AML authorities are automatically declined.
* The local on-premise PostgreSQL database maintains the dynamic list of blocked Merchant Category Codes (MCCs) and country IP ranges. Any RAG query determining transaction validity must cross-reference this local table.
* Under no circumstances can a frontline support agent override a national geographic blocklist.
