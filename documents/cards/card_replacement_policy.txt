## 1. Card Replacement Fees
Fees for replacing lost, stolen, or physically damaged cards apply based on the customer tier. Expired cards are replaced automatically at no charge.

* Standard Tier: 35 SAR (Exclusive of 15% VAT).
* Premium Tier: 0 SAR (Fee waived automatically by billing system).
* VIP Tier: 0 SAR (Fee waived automatically).

## 2. Issuance and Delivery SLAs
Customers may choose between physical delivery or self-service printing.

* Self-Service Kiosk: Immediate issuance. Customer must authenticate via National ID and fingerprint biometrics at the terminal.
* Courier Delivery (Domestic): 3 to 5 business days. Dispatched only to the verified National Address on file.
* Courier Delivery (International): Reserved for VIP tier only. Expedited global shipping via DHL within 4 to 7 business days.
