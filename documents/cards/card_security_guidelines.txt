## 1. Automated Fraud Detection Triggers
The local fraud detection engine evaluates transaction telemetry in real-time. The following events trigger an automatic, immediate card block (Code: 62 - Restricted Card).

* Velocity Trigger: More than 4 distinct POS or E-commerce transactions within a 5-minute rolling window.
* Geographic Anomaly: A physical POS transaction in a foreign country within 4 hours of an ATM withdrawal in Saudi Arabia.
* PIN Failure: 3 consecutive incorrect PIN entries at any ATM or POS terminal.
* CVV Failure: 3 consecutive incorrect CVV entries during online checkout.

## 2. 3D Secure (3DS) Mandates
All E-commerce transactions must pass through the bank's local 3D Secure gateway.
* If the merchant platform does not support 3DS, the transaction is automatically declined.
* OTP Expiry for 3DS: 120 seconds.
* Overriding 3DS with static passwords is strictly prohibited.
