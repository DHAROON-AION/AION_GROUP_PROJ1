## 1. Credit Card Fee Structure
This policy outlines the standardized fees and charges applicable to all credit card products across standard and premium tiers.

* Annual Membership Fee: Standard Tier is 200 SAR; Premium Tier is 500 SAR (waived for the first year).
* Late Payment Fee: 100 SAR per billing cycle if the minimum payment (5% of the outstanding balance or 100 SAR, whichever is higher) is not received by the 25th of the month.
* Cash Advance Fee: 75 SAR per transaction or 3% of the advanced amount, whichever is greater, up to a maximum of 30% of the total assigned credit limit.
* Foreign Transaction Fee: 2.75% markup on the standard Visa/Mastercard exchange rate.

## 2. Credit Limit Assignment
* Minimum Credit Limit: 5,000 SAR.
* Maximum Credit Limit: Capped at 3 times the customer's verified monthly net salary, subject to the central bank's maximum allowable Debt Burden Ratio (DBR) of 33%.
