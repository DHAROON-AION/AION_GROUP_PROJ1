## 1. Accrual Rates for Cashback Credit Cards
The automated loyalty engine calculates cashback daily and posts the accumulated total on the billing cycle date. Accrual rates are mapped to specific Merchant Category Codes (MCC).

* Supermarkets & Groceries (MCC 5411): 2.0% Cashback
* Fuel & Gas Stations (MCC 5541, 5542): 1.5% Cashback
* International E-Commerce (Non-SAR currency): 1.0% Cashback
* All other domestic retail spend: 0.5% Cashback
* Exclusions: Utility bill payments (SADAD), government fees, cash advances, and crypto-currency purchases accrue 0.0% cashback.

## 2. Redemption Rules
* Minimum Redemption Threshold: 100 SAR.
* Maximum Monthly Cap: 1,500 SAR per billing cycle.
* Cash is credited directly to the card's outstanding statement balance; it cannot be transferred to a current or savings account.
