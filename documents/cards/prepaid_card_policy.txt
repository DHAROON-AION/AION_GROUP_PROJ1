## 1. Prepaid Card Characteristics
Prepaid cards are strictly pay-as-you-go instruments. They are not linked directly to a depository checking account and offer zero overdraft or credit facilities.

* Maximum Load Balance: The total stored value on a prepaid card cannot exceed 10,000 SAR at any given time.
* Monthly Reload Limit: Capped at 20,000 SAR per calendar month.
* Minimum Reload Amount: 50 SAR per transaction.

## 2. KYC and Issuance Restrictions
* Even though prepaid cards do not offer credit, full KYC (Know Your Customer) identity verification via the national ID registry is legally mandated prior to issuance.
* Anonymous or "bearer" prepaid cards are strictly prohibited under national anti-money laundering frameworks.
* Users may maintain a maximum of two active prepaid cards per CIF (Customer Information File).
