## 1. Debit Card Transaction Limits
The following daily limits apply to standard and premium debit cards linked to current or savings accounts. Limits reset at 00:00 AST daily.

### 1.1 Standard Debit Card (Mada / Visa)
* ATM Daily Cash Withdrawal Limit: 5,000 SAR
* Point of Sale (POS) Daily Limit: 20,000 SAR
* E-Commerce Daily Limit: 20,000 SAR (Requires manual activation via the app)

### 1.2 Premium/VIP Debit Card (Visa Infinite / Mastercard World)
* ATM Daily Cash Withdrawal Limit: 20,000 SAR (Subject to branch liquidity rules)
* Point of Sale (POS) Daily Limit: 200,000 SAR
* E-Commerce Daily Limit: 100,000 SAR

## 2. Limit Modification Rules
* POS and E-commerce limits can be reduced to 0 SAR by the user via the mobile app for immediate security.
* ATM withdrawal limits cannot exceed 5,000 SAR for Standard Tier accounts under any circumstances without physical branch override and dual biometric authentication.
