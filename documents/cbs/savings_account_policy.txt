Title: Savings Account Policy

Purpose

The AION Bank Savings Account is designed to encourage personal savings while providing customers with secure and convenient banking services. This policy outlines the eligibility criteria, account features, operational rules, and minimum balance requirements for savings account holders.

Eligibility

Any Pakistani citizen aged 18 years or above with a valid CNIC may apply for a savings account. Overseas Pakistanis may also open an account subject to applicable regulations and documentation.

Required Documents

- Original CNIC
- Proof of address
- Recent passport-sized photograph
- Source of income declaration (if required)

Minimum Initial Deposit

Customers must deposit a minimum of PKR 5,000 at the time of account opening.

Minimum Monthly Balance

A minimum balance of PKR 5,000 must be maintained at all times. Failure to maintain the required balance may result in applicable service charges according to the bank's schedule of charges.

Profit Rate

Savings accounts earn profit at an annual rate of 9%, calculated on the average monthly balance and credited according to the bank's profit distribution schedule.

Available Services

- ATM/Debit Card
- Internet Banking
- Mobile Banking
- SMS Alerts
- Optional Cheque Book
- Utility Bill Payments
- Funds Transfer

Dormancy

Accounts with no customer-initiated financial transaction for twelve consecutive months will be classified as dormant. Dormant accounts can be reactivated after successful customer verification.

Notes

AION Bank reserves the right to revise this policy in accordance with regulations issued by the State Bank of Pakistan.
