## 1. Issuance Criteria
Cheque books are strictly issued to Current Account holders. Savings accounts are ineligible for cheque book facilities.
* First Issuance: A standard 10-leaf cheque book is issued free of charge upon account opening if requested.
* Subsequent Requests: 25 SAR per 25-leaf book for Standard Tier customers. The fee is waived for Premium and VIP customers.
* Restrictions: Accounts with a history of two or more returned (bounced) cheques due to insufficient funds within a 12-month period are permanently barred from requesting new cheque books.

## 2. Stop Payment Orders
Customers may issue a "Stop Payment" on a specific cheque or range of cheques via the mobile app or branch.
* Fee: 50 SAR per stop payment request.
* Validity: The stop payment mandate is active in the core system for 180 days.
