Title: Current Account Policy

Purpose

The AION Bank Current Account is designed for individuals and businesses requiring frequent financial transactions without profit earnings. This policy defines the account features, operational guidelines, and customer responsibilities.

Eligibility

Current accounts may be opened by individuals, sole proprietors, partnerships, companies, and organizations after successful verification.

Required Documents

- Original CNIC
- Business registration documents (where applicable)
- Proof of address
- Tax registration documents (for businesses)

Minimum Initial Deposit

PKR 10,000

Minimum Monthly Balance

Customers are required to maintain a minimum balance of PKR 10,000.

Profit

Current accounts do not earn profit.

Cheque Book

Customers receive a complimentary cheque book upon successful account activation. Additional cheque books may be requested according to the bank's schedule of charges.

Overdraft Facility

Eligible customers may apply for overdraft facilities subject to credit assessment and management approval.

Available Services

- Unlimited deposits
- ATM/Debit Card
- Internet Banking
- Mobile Banking
- Online Fund Transfers
- Salary Processing

Notes

AION Bank may modify account features in accordance with State Bank of Pakistan regulations.
