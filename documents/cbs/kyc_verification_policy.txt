Title: KYC Verification Policy

Purpose

Know Your Customer (KYC) is a mandatory process used by AION Bank to verify customer identity and comply with anti-money laundering (AML) and counter financing of terrorism (CFT) regulations.

Required Documents

- Original CNIC
- Proof of address
- Recent passport-sized photograph
- Source of income declaration when required

Verification Process

Customer information is verified using official identification documents and internal compliance procedures. Additional verification may be requested for high-risk customers.

Risk Classification

Customers are classified into Low, Medium, or High risk categories based on occupation, transaction profile, and regulatory requirements.

Compliance

No account may be activated until KYC verification has been successfully completed. The bank performs periodic reviews to ensure customer information remains accurate and up to date.

Incomplete KYC

Accounts with incomplete KYC documentation may experience restrictions including transaction limits, temporary suspension, or closure if required documentation is not provided within the specified period.

Customer Responsibility

Customers are responsible for informing AION Bank of any changes to their personal information, address, employment status, or identification documents.

Notes

This policy complies with the Know Your Customer and Anti-Money Laundering regulations issued by the State Bank of Pakistan.
