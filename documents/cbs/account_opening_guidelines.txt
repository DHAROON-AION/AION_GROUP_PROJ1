Title: Account Opening Guidelines

Purpose

These guidelines define the standard process for opening customer accounts at AION Bank. The objective is to ensure compliance with banking regulations while providing customers with an efficient onboarding experience.

Eligibility

Applicants must meet the eligibility requirements of the selected account type and provide valid identification documents.

Required Documents

- Original CNIC
- Proof of residential address
- Recent passport-sized photograph
- Source of income declaration where applicable

Account Opening Process

1. Submit the completed account opening form.
2. Provide all required supporting documents.
3. Complete customer verification.
4. Perform KYC screening.
5. Deposit the required initial amount.
6. Receive account number and welcome package.

Verification

Bank staff verify customer identity using official identification documents and internal verification procedures before account approval.

Account Activation

Once verification is successfully completed, the account is activated and customers receive access to ATM, mobile banking, and internet banking services.

Processing Time

Under normal circumstances, account opening is completed within one to three working days.

Notes

Applications containing incomplete or inaccurate information may be delayed or rejected until the required information is provided.
