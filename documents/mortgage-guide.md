# AION Bank — Mortgage and Home Finance

## Eligibility
- Minimum salary: SAR 8,000 for nationals, SAR 12,000 for expatriates
- Maximum age at maturity: 65 years
- Maximum finance amount: 85% of property value for first home

## Finance Types
- Murabaha home finance (Sharia-compliant)
- Conventional mortgage (selected properties)

## Tenure
Up to 25 years depending on age and income.

## Rates
Indicative profit rates: 3.5% to 6.5% depending on loan-to-value ratio and customer profile.

## Property Requirements
Property must be valued by AION-approved surveyor. Insurance is mandatory for financed properties.

## Early Settlement
Early settlement permitted with 1% penalty on outstanding balance if settled within first 3 years.
